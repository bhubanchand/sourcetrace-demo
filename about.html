OUR STORY

SourceTrace was founded with a vision to use technology to make agriculture more sustainable, efficient, and equitable. Starting with financial inclusion, we pivoted to digital agriculture to address the pressing needs of farmers in developing economies.

MISSION & VISION

We aim to empower farmers and organizations by providing full visibility into the agricultural value chain. Our DATAGREEN platform connects millions of farmers to global markets, ensuring fair trade and environmental sustainability.
