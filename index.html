Bridging the Gap in the Last Mile.

Managing a scattered agricultural value chain is complex. Lack of data, low visibility, and manual processes lead to leakage and inefficiency.

SourceTrace DATAGREEN™ brings every stakeholder onto one unified platform.

Comprehensive Solutions

Farmer Profiling (KYC)

Capture complete socio-economic profiles, GPS location of farms, and family data to ensure targeted support and authentic sourcing.

Crop Advisory & Alerts

Send real-time pest, disease, and weather alerts directly to farmers’ mobile phones to mitigate risks and improve yield.

Certification Management

Simplify compliance for Organic, Fairtrade, and Rainforest Alliance certifications with automated Internal Control Systems (ICS).

Traceability & Blockchain

Guarantee provenance with farm-to-retail traceability. Generate QR codes that tell the full story of your produce.

Farm-to-Fork Analytics

Visualize yield estimates, procurement data, and supply chain bottlenecks on a centralized, real-time dashboard.

Financial Services

Facilitate credit access, crop insurance, and digital payments directly to farmer bank accounts, ensuring financial inclusion.

How It Works

1. Enrollment

Field agents register farmers and map plots using GPS on the SourceTrace App (Works Offline).

2. Cultivation

Farmers receive advisory; field staff monitor crop growth and input usage.

3. Harvest & Procurement

Digital weighing and receipts are generated instantly at the collection center.

4. Processing & Logistics

Produce is tracked through warehousing and transport with batch numbers.

5. Retail

Consumers scan a QR code to see the farmer's face and the journey of their food.
